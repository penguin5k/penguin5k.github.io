Penguin in the Park 5K Run\/Walk will be celebrating its 11th anniversary on \u00a0March 19, 2016! The Penguin will be making his only public appearance at\u00a0his alma mater, Millikin University, in Decatur, Illinois. This is a race that embraces the Penguin in all of us\u2014a race for the speedy, and those who want to take their time and enjoy the course!<\/p>\n

Starting at 9:00am, the 5K takes place in Fairview Park on a rolling bike path; afterwards, we move to the post-race party inside to Millikin\u2019s Griswold Gym for awards, warm food, and inspiration from the Penguin.<\/p>\n

New to the event this year is a 300 meter Tot Trot to get youth more involved in a lifelong activity. Tot Trot participants will receive a t-shirt and goody bag as well as a finisher ribbon. Make Penguin in the Park a family event this year.<\/p>\n
