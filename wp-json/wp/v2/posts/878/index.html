For all you Penguins out there in the Decatur area, the Tuesday Night Park Runs start next Tuesday, June 12 in Fairview Park. Entry fee is still just $1 and supports the Staley Striders. You can sign up beginning at 5:30. The 1\/2 mile Tot Trot begins at 6:00, with the 1 and 3 mile beginning at 6:15.<\/p>\n

We hope to see you there. You can download a schedule using the link below.<\/p>\n
