We’re excited to have you participate in the 12th Penguin in the Park 5K on Saturday, March 25. This email contains important information about the race weekend.<\/h4>\n
    \n
  • The 5K will begin promptly at 9:00 am.<\/li>\n
  • Move\u00a0towards the start line by 8:50 for the national anthem, prayer, and pre-race instructions.<\/li>\n
  • The 300 meter Penguin Dash will begin at 8:30. The little penguins will be\u00a0led to the start line. Meet by the bathrooms near the bike bath at 8:15.<\/li>\n
  • The forecast looks like it could get soggy. Come prepared as the event will go on rain or shine. Penguins like to get wet \ud83d\ude42 The race directors will make a determination to delay, postpone, or cancel the race if participants’ safety is at risk, due to events such as lightning.<\/li>\n
  • The race is electronically timed by a “chip” that is integrated into your bib number. You must wear your bib on your front across your stomach (and visible)<\/strong>. Your bib DOES NOT have to be returned or the tag torn when you cross through the chute and is disposable. Remember, no bib… NO TIME!<\/li>\n
  • You can still register for the race on race day between 7 am and 8:30 am.<\/li>\n
  • Race course map<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Packet Pick Up<\/em><\/span><\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

      \n
    • Pick up your race packet on Friday at Staley Credit Union<\/a> on N. Woodford in Decatur between 11:00 am and 5:00 pm.<\/strong>\u00a0click here for directions<\/a>.<\/li>\n
    • Participants who pick up their packet on Friday are automatically entered in a drawing for a $25 Visa gift card. Ray’s Chilli will also be on site selling chilli dogs so you can grab lunch on the go.<\/li>\n
    • Race packets may also be picked up prior to the race on Saturday between 7:15 am and 8:30 am.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      Parking and Pre-Race<\/em><\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

        \n
      • We are expecting a lot of folks, so parking will be difficult. We recommend that you park in one of the Millikin parking lots along Fairview Ave (in front of Kirkland Fine Arts Center or next to Westminster Church).\u00a0Click here for campus map.<\/a>\u00a0This will allow you easy access to both Fairview Park and Griswold Gym for the post-race party with a little warm-up and cool down walk to and from both locations.<\/li>\n
      • Prior to the race, Coffee Connection<\/a> will be on site selling hot drinks to keep you warm and gear you up for the race.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        Post-Race Party<\/span><\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n

          \n
        • When you are finished with the race, grab a cup of water and head over to Millikin’s Griswold Center for the post race party where you’ll find good food, water provided by Pepsi, pasta, and famous Ray’s Chili.<\/li>\n
        • Check out the prize table<\/strong> to see if you have won a gift certificate or other goodies provided by our generous sponsors.<\/li>\n
        • Vintage Penguin in the Park 5K items will be for sale like t-shirts and race bags for $5.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

          Remember that “The miracle isn’t that you finished. It’s that you had the courage to start.”<\/p>\n

          We look forward to having you in Fairview Park this weekend and we’re excited about another great Penguin in the Park.<\/p>\n

          Happy Waddling,
